AARO Historical Record Report Vol. 1: What It Actually Says

In March 2024, the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) released its first Historical Record Report — a congressionally mandated review of U.S. government UAP programs dating back to 1945. It is the most comprehensive official accounting of UAP-related activities ever made public. Here is what it actually says, sourced directly from the document. What AARO Concluded The report’s central finding is direct: AARO found no verifiable evidence that the U.S. government has recovered non-human intelligence, extraterrestrial spacecraft, or reverse-engineered alien technology.
